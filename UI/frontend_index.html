Relevant Statutory References

Section 2(11)
"Defect"
Means any fault, imperfection or shortcoming in the quality, quantity, potency, purity or standard which is required to be maintained by or under any law for the time being in force.
Section 2(47)
"Unfair Trade Practice"
Includes a trade practice which, for the purpose of promoting the sale, use or supply of any goods... adopts any unfair method or deceptive practice.
Section 35
Filing a Complaint
Outlines the jurisdiction and process by which a consumer can file a complaint with the District Commission.
